- **What I accomplished**:

  - Established game balance: 18 space track, 25 HP Boss, 12-15 minute playtime target
  
- **Challenges faced**:
  - Initial game design was too similar to Spoons.
  - Boss felt passive and unengaged, just a moving health bar
  - Discovered exploit where players could cooperate to kill Boss quickly then race without interaction
  
- **Solutions**:
  - Added board/racing element to create spatial progression and strategic tension
  - Made Boss an active player with unique actions (Attack and Block Path) instead of being a passive obstacle
  - Designed low Boss HP (25) but high impact actions to compensate for winning fewer rounds
    
#### Learnings
- Depth vs Complexity: Learned to create meaningful choices (depth) without overcomplicating rules (complexity) 
- Asymmetric Balance: Boss role needs different power level and actions to compensate for probability disadvantage
- Emergent Gameplay: Simultaneous card matching + strategic action choice creates unscripted moments and comeback mechanics
